How it works in 3

simple steps


Set Up Your Property

Quickly add your properties and units within the app. Whether you manage a single building or multiple properties, Propty allows you to scale effortlessly.

Create and Assign Bills

Generate bills with just a few clicks. Assign them to specific tenants, set due dates, and automate recurring bills. Propty takes care of sending reminders, ensuring timely payments.

Manage and Collect Payments

Tenants can view and pay their bills online through a secure payment gateway. Track all transactions and access comprehensive reports to monitor your cash flow and outstanding balances.
